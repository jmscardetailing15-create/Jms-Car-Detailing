About Us

JMS Car Detailing was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality auto detailing services using premium products and techniques. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a team of dedicated professionals who share a passion for automotive excellence.

Our team consists of certified detailers with years of experience in both traditional and innovative detailing methods. We continuously invest in training and the latest technology to ensure we deliver exceptional results for every vehicle we service.

We take pride in our attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction. Whether you drive a daily commuter or a luxury sports car, we treat every vehicle with the care and precision it deserves.
